Day 3

04.08.15

Time for a little abstraction. When I prepared my usual tea and oatmeal this morning, I boiled some extra water to make these emulsion lifts. An emulsion lift is the removal and transfer of the emulsion layer of Polaroid film. It disconnects from the rigid square sheet and becomes a sort of jelly, flowing image that can stick to other things. These came from some old developed but unexposed film that otherwise would've been wasted.

Day 2

04.07.15

Call me selfish, but for the past several months I've been focusing a lot on giving to myself. Whether that's going to bed early instead of staying out with friends or taking a day off work for a treat-yourself day trip, I'm giving in to what I want and learning why I do. I feel clearer in who I am and what I can have. It's easier to find the time and energy for the big things when you gift yourself the little things instead of stressing about them all.

Though it has been a couple of months since I moved into a new house, a lot of it stills feels mysterious to me. There are nooks, like this damp passageway, I've yet to explore and creaks that still creep me out when heard home alone at night.
