Day 3

04.08.15

Time for a little abstraction. When I prepared my usual tea and oatmeal this morning, I boiled some extra water to make these emulsion lifts. An emulsion lift is the removal and transfer of the emulsion layer of Polaroid film. It disconnects from the rigid square sheet and becomes a sort of jelly, flowing image that can stick to other things. These came from some old developed but unexposed film that otherwise would've been wasted.
